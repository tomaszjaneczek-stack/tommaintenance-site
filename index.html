Compliance

Built for UK construction sites: onboarding, induction, RAMS and COSHH.

Site readiness

  • CSCS: Green card process (CITB test to be booked)
  • RAMS: Method statements available per site scope
  • COSHH: Product assessments and safe storage/handling
  • Insurance: Public Liability / Employers’ Liability (as required)
  • Induction: We follow contractor onboarding & site rules

We do not claim compliance items as “completed” unless they are confirmed and documented.

Accreditations

  • CHAS: in progress (sole trader route)
  • We can work to your SSOW / permit system if applicable
  • PPE and signage aligned to site requirements
  • Working around other trades with minimal disruption
